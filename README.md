## Introduction

Jahannam, often translated as Hell, is a concept found in various religious traditions. It is typically described as a place of punishment or torment for those who have committed major sins or transgressions. This repository aims to compile a curated list of currently living evil doer's who's sin are major and verified as they will be residents of Jahannam from different religious texts and learning.

The list may include kannas (Those who does magic), big kafir's (Included other with creator), deceivers, and individuals who are said to will be condemned to Hell in various religious narratives. Each entry in the list will provide information about the entity or person, their association with Jahannam, and relevant references from religious texts or from their living and act's.
